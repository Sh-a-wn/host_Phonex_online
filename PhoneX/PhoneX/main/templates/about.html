ABOUT US

Mission:

Our aim is to satisfy all the needs users may have when it comes to purchasing or selling phones by gathering buyers and sellers in a single, easy-to-use platform where phones can be exchanged, hence the name Phonex.

How to buy devices on Phonex:

To buy phones:

  • Login to your account or create an account if you don't already have one.

  • Switch your account role to 'Buyer' from the Roles page.

  • Vist our Deals page and browse through the available brands.

  • If you see something you like, feel free to add it to your cart and checkout.


    • How to sell devices on Phonex:

    To sell a phone:

  • Switch your account role to 'Seller' from the Roles page.

  • Visit our Sell Phone page and correctly fill in the details about the phone you would like to sell.

    • Note: The fields 'Name' and 'Price' cannot be left blank but it is advised to fill in all fields for the best results.

  • After filling in and submitting the form, you may visit the Deals page to have a look at your device.

    • Seller Guidelines:

    Pay attention to the details.

    Use good photos of your device.

    Write a clear and detailed descriptions about your device.

    Ensure you have full legal ownership of the device you are selling. Failure to meet this requirement can lead to legal action being taken.
